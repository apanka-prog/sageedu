Meet Lisa Panka

Strategy rooted in 25+ years inside education

Lisa Panka started her career as a preschool and elementary classroom teacher before moving into education publishing. She has carried that student-first perspective into every role since.

In her 25+ years in publishing and EdTech, Lisa has done nearly everything: writing and editing content, building departments from scratch, and serving on senior leadership teams helping to shape companies' futures. She founded Sage Education Consulting to bring that same practitioner's perspective to the organizations navigating this industry.
