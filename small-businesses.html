Small Businesses

Nearly half of all private-sector workers in the U.S. work for a small business, and the vast majority of those businesses have fewer than 50 employees. Small businesses are the backbone of the economy — and they deserve strategic guidance too. Whether you're launching a new business, moving into growth mode, or working to stabilize what you've already built, Sage is here to help.

How Sage Can Help

  • Strategizing and planning for company growth and scaling
  • Identifying blockers within your organization and finding solutions
  • Spotting growth areas for your organization
  • Coaching on developing strong client relationships
  • Identifying the key roles your organization needs, with guidance on hiring and promoting from within
  • Conducting competitor analysis

If any of this sounds like where your business is right now, let's talk about what's next.
