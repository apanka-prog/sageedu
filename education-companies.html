EdTech Companies

EdTech companies face a similar set of pressures to publishers — funding cycles, shifting state regulations, and the ongoing work of proving your product's value to schools and districts. Sage helps you build the internal processes and vendor relationships that support long-term growth.

Challenges We Help You Navigate

  • Maintaining product adoption as school funding changes
  • Navigating regulatory differences from state to state
  • Identifying new revenue streams
  • Vetting and selecting the right implementation and content vendors
  • Writing RFPs and RFIs that produce comparable, usable vendor responses
  • Evaluating vendor proposals and samples
  • Designing workflows that increase efficiency without sacrificing quality
  • Building durable, collaborative vendor partnerships
